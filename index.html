# DS2 Page Title

Do certain factors affect influence score more than others?

This graph is important because it showcases the amount of average likes, therefore shows how many people are being influenced by these celebrities and if you should also be influenced by said celebrities. The question I used was "Is there a positive, statistically significant correlation between the average likes an influencer has on a post and their influence score?” I chose to use the average number of likes and the celebrities’ influencer score to find out how many people are usually viewing these people’s posts. Through the data provided, you can see a fairly strong association between the average number of likes and the influence score that each celebrity has. This means that you can accurately predict how much influence a celebrity has by the amount of average likes they get per post. Some threats to validity are being left out like how much they post, when they made their account, or what they posted.
